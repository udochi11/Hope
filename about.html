About Us

Hope in the Jungle Initiative (HITJI) is a Non-profit Organization Dedicated to Empowering and nurturing Hope in Children, Youth, and Women from Crime-prone and underserved Communities. Despite growing up amidst Poverty, violence, crime and other Forms of Adversity, have shown Remarkable Resilience and strength. We aim to provide Opportunities for these Vulnerable Individuals through, Educational support, Training, Capacity-building, and Community Development, thereby fostering resilience, Academic Excellence, Peaceful Coexistence and sustainable Futures through Collective Action and support from individuals, Organizations, and Corporations.

Hope in the Jungle Initiative (HITJI) works in Troubled Communities Plagued by Heavy Presence of Cult War, Militancy, Agitation and Other Forms of Crime as a Result of Long-term Neglect of the People and the devastating effect of Oil Pollution. It provides Tangible Opportunities for these Vulnerable Individuals, Fostering Resilience, Capacity Building, and peaceful coexistence.
The Organization HITJI is a deliberate Action Platform, focused on addressing the critical Physical, social and Environmental Challenges Facing Children, Youths, and Women in most Niger Delta Communities. The initiative Addresses several aligned Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), of SDG 1: No Poverty; SDG 4: Quality Education; SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities; SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions. We Believe that Every One deserves the Chance to Thrive and Build a positive future.
Hope in the jungle Initiative, is incorporate Affairs Commission (CAC).
