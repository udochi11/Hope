What We Do

Hope in the Jungle Initiative scholarship Scheme is driven by a passion and commitment to empowering the neglected brilliant indigent and deprived children and youths of vulnerable Niger Delta communities. Many of these children and youths have grown up amidst poverty, militancy, and other forms of crime but have shown remarkable resilience and faith, through open scholarships and capacity-building programs.. To inspire the belief that a better future is possible through collective action. The Hope in the Jungle initiative launches the Master’s Degree scholarship project for brilliant students from the troubled Niger Delta communities, starting from Elibrada, with the aim of encouraging scholarship for global participation. It is designed to recognize and encourage potential global scholars and leaders in Niger Delta. It is established to promote academic and professional excellence, and create a platform for respected individuals or organisations to empower talented young individuals in the Niger Delta
The scholarships are instituted in honour of respected members from the community in recognition of their impactful contribution to the educational and socio-economic development of their communities. These esteemed community members called ’ICONs’ have played pivotal roles in the socioeconomic upliftment of their local communities.

Icons

Icons are either individuals that have distinguished themselves in their chosen fields, or made significant impact in human capital development, or are source of great inspiration for their communities. ICONs are not necessarily prominent and rich but individuals with auditable records and positive legacies of honour.

Eligibity

Eligibility is based on achievements in promotion examinations, and other significant academic accomplishments. Appliicants must be indigenes of Niger Delta communities, and must have minimum CGPA requirements with admission letters to pursue a regular programme in a public university in Nigeria

Duration

• Masters is for a session not more than 18 months with lumpsum payment
• PhD is for the duration of the programme, with lumpsum payment.
• Special needs- lumpsum on one-off or negotiated.

Target recipients

Bachelor or Master Degree holders from Niger Delta communities with outstanding academic achievement of a minimum of 4 point in a 5 point-scale or 5.5point in a 7point-scale in any course from a public university in Nigeria, and doing the program in a Nigerian public university.
